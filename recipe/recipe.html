Our Signature Recipes

1. Jollof Rice with Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of long-grain rice
  • 4 fresh tomatoes (blended)
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • Spices (salt, thyme, curry, pepper)
  • Fried chicken pieces

Preparation:

  1. Heat oil in a pot and fry onions and tomato paste for 3 minutes.
  2. Add blended tomatoes and cook until thickened.
  3. Pour in chicken stock and seasonings; bring to a boil.
  4. Add rice and cook on low heat until soft.
  5. Serve with fried chicken and garnish with vegetables.

2. Grilled Fish with Fries

Ingredients:

  • 1 whole tilapia or catfish (cleaned)
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 lemon
  • Fries (to serve)

Preparation:

  1. Mix oil, paprika, pepper, and salt into a paste.
  2. Rub mixture over the fish and let it marinate for 30 minutes.
  3. Grill until golden brown on both sides.
  4. Serve hot with fries and lemon wedges.
